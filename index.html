Women in ECS

About Us

Women in ECS is a society at the University of Southampton dedicated to supporting and empowering women in the field of Electronics and Computer Science.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive environment where women in technology can connect, grow, and succeed through networking events, workshops, and mentorship programs.

What We Do

We organize technical workshops, career development sessions, networking events with industry professionals, and social gatherings to build a strong community.

Join Us

Whether you're a current student, alumni, or industry professional, we welcome you to join our community and help us shape the future of women in technology.
